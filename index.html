A Prairie Blueprint for the 21st Century

Saskatchewan has always built for itself. The Initiative gathers residents, newcomers, and partners to modernize every system that touches daily life: housing, health, income, education, transport, energy, and the relationship between provincial and Indigenous governments. Every bill works in concert to guarantee human dignity and practical prosperity for generations.
