The Strategy

The Xanatos Gambit

A plan where every possible outcome strengthens Saskatchewan.

Most political parties treat their platform like a wish list. We do not. The Saskatchewan 21 was designed as a system where each piece reinforces the others, where opposition creates openings, and where public ownership gives this province more room to act.

Sure, it is an unusual name for a political strategy. We aren't your usual Party. Judge us on whether it works.
