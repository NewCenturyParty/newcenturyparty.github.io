Who We Need

Help Build
The New Century

The New Century Party (NCP) has a strong foundation: Interlocking 21 Policies, an honest 25 Year Fiscal Framework, and a Xanatos Gambit that will build it all against the odds.

What the NCP does not have is you.

If you have ever watched a resource boom pass through your town without leaving much behind...

If you have ever watched services cut while the province exports billions...

If you have ever known in your gut that something is wrong with how this place is run...


Then you already understand what this party is about.

Saskatchewan has never been short of wealth. It has been short of a government willing to keep it and use it for regular people here, not a distant shareholder.

We are not looking for the expert.
We are not looking for the politician.
We are not looking for the professional.
Though all are welcome.

We are looking for people who still believe, despite everything, that Saskatchewan can be better than this. People willing to fight for it.

We are looking for you
