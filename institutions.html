Public Builders

Institutions

Crown corporations old and new deliver the Initiative. Each has a statute-backed mandate, transparent dashboards, and co-governance obligations.

New Crowns & Agencies

Built to close gaps the private market ignored—from public groceries to digital services.

Saskatchewan Grocery (SaskGrocery)

Cost-plus grocery co-ops with rooftop greenhouses, mobile markets, and cold-chain logistics tied to STC.

Saskatchewan Agriculture Board (SAB)

Sets floor prices, manages regenerative dividends, and operates transparent wholesale auctions.

Saskatchewan Digital Services (SDS)

Designs open-source platforms, SaskOS, and the git.saskatchewan.ca civic repository.

Saskatchewan Century Corps (SCC)

A skills corps that trains and deploys people in housing, care, climate, and digital projects.

Saskatchewan Manufacturing Corporation (SMC)

Builds prefab homes, heat pumps, and renewable hardware with worker-centered schedules.

Renewed Mandates

Existing Crowns get clearer goals, climate targets, and public reporting.

Saskatchewan Housing Corporation (SHC)

Acquires, builds, and retrofits homes with SaskHomes plans and 21-day permit clocks.

Saskatchewan Transport Corporation (STC)

Returns as a province-wide bus and logistics network with cold-chain equipped depots.

Saskatchewan Power & SaskEnergy

Deliver ambient thermal loops, microgrids, and 2055 clean power mandates.

Saskatchewan Telecommunications (SaskTel)

Completes fibre-to-every-home builds and runs public email with privacy guarantees.

Saskatchewan Government Insurance (SGI)

Adds housing, disaster, and renters insurance with climate-resilience incentives.

Accountability & Oversight

Saskatchewan Dignity Fund Authority (SDFA)

Manages the Dignity Fund with 36-month liquidity tranches, fossil-free investments, and quarterly dashboards.

Saskatchewan Council for Reconciliation (SCR)

Co-governs with Indigenous nations, issues binding directions, and administers the Consent Protocol registry.

Saskatchewan Emergency Response Agency (SERA)

Coordinates resilience hubs, all-hazard planning, and parametric insurance triggers with SGI.
