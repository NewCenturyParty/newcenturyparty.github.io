Questions

Frequently Asked

Quick answers to the questions residents ask most. Have something we missed? Reach out—we ship updates often.

How is the Initiative funded?

The Initiative uses multiple tools: Crown dividends, progressive tax brackets, the Universal Health Levy, Sask Bonds, and the Saskatchewan Dignity Fund. Each bill includes transparent costing and dashboards.

What happens to existing private services?

Residents keep choice—but public options must be universal, cheaper, and transparent. Private providers can opt into public billing if they honour direct billing and zero co-pay rules.

How does Free, Prior, and Informed Consent work here?

The Saskatchewan Reconciliation & Treaty Renewal Act requires a Consent Protocol co-developed with affected Indigenous governments and SCR. Projects pause until agreements are reached.

Can I contribute edits or raise concerns?

Yes. Fork the GitHub repo, open an issue with evidence or drafts, or email hello@newcenturyparty.ca. Policy notes from residents, unions, and businesses shape revisions.

Where can I see progress?

Every bill mandates public dashboards. Prototype dashboards and datasets will ship alongside the first implementation schedule—check the Get Involved page for updates.
